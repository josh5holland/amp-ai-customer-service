# Fuel Kitchen - AMP's On-Site Restaurant

## What This Is
Fuel Kitchen is the on-site restaurant at Atlanta Motorsports Park. It's relevant to group events because we recommend pairing karting packages with dining options.

## Location & Contact
- **Location:** On-site at AMP, Joe Lane Cox Road, Dawsonville, GA 30534
- **Phone:** (678) 288-4244

## Hours
- **Wednesday - Sunday:** 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- **Monday & Tuesday:** CLOSED (same as AMP group events)

## About Fuel Kitchen

### Concept
Chef-driven, farm-to-table restaurant specializing in elevated comfort food with Southern influences. Not your typical track-side concession stand.

### Food Quality Highlights
- **Burgers:** Hand-pressed, never frozen, cooked in beef tallow and clarified butter (no seed oils)
- **Chicken:** Locally-sourced, hormone-free, marinated overnight, hand-breaded
- **Ingredients:** Fresh, locally-sourced meats, artisan breads, seasonal produce

### Executive Chef
Jason Perry - background includes country club dining and Ruth's Chris Steakhouse. Focuses on combining local ingredients with refined technique.

## Group Event Catering

Fuel Kitchen is the **exclusive caterer** for all AMP-hosted events. This is important for group karting packages.

### Catering Options
- Breakfast/brunch service
- Hors d'oeuvres
- Food stations
- Buffet service
- Carving stations
- Gourmet displays

### Recommendations for Group Events
- **Groups under 20:** Recommend dining a la carte at Fuel Kitchen
- **Groups of 20+:** Recommend buffet catering option
- **All groups:** Can pair with private room rental for a complete experience
