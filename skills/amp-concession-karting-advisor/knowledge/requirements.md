# Concession Karting Requirements

## Age & Height Requirements

### Standard Rental Karts
| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Age | 12 years old |
| Height | 60 inches (5 feet) |

**Both requirements must be met.**

### Minors (Under 18)
- Waiver must be signed by parent/guardian
- Adult must be present at facility during session

### Junior Discovery (Alternative for Kids)
- Ages: 5-11 years old
- No height requirement
- Separate program (not rental karts)

## What Customers Must Bring/Wear

### Required
- **Closed-toe shoes** - Sandals, flip-flops, and open-toe shoes NOT allowed
- No loose clothing that could get caught in kart

### Recommended
- **Long pants** - Protects legs in kart seat
- **Tie back long hair** - Keeps hair out of way and safe

### Optional
- Personal helmet (if preferred over loaners)
- Gloves (not necessary but some prefer)

---

## Safety Briefing

Before driving, all participants must:
1. Complete waiver (minors need parent/guardian signature)
2. Watch/receive safety briefing
3. Understand flag signals and track rules

**This is mandatory** - no one drives without completing the briefing.

## Eligibility Check

### Can They Drive Rental Karts?

```
Is driver 12+ years old?
├── Yes → Is driver 60+ inches tall?
│         ├── Yes → ELIGIBLE for rental karts
│         └── No → NOT ELIGIBLE (height requirement)
└── No → Is driver 5-11 years old?
          ├── Yes → ELIGIBLE for Junior Discovery only
          └── No → NOT ELIGIBLE (too young)
```

### Common Scenarios

| Scenario | Eligible? | Alternative |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| 14 years old, 5'4" | Yes | - |
| 11 years old, 5'0" | No | Junior Discovery |
| 13 years old, 4'10" | No | Wait until taller |
| 8 years old, any height | No | Junior Discovery |
| 4 years old | No | No programs available |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "Can we bring our own helmets?"
Yes, personal helmets are allowed. They should be appropriate karting/motorcycle helmets.

### "What if my child is 12 but short?"
Both age AND height requirements must be met. If under 60 inches, they cannot drive rental karts regardless of age.

### "Are there karts for younger kids?"
Our rental karts are for 12+. For kids 5-11, we offer the Junior Discovery Experience (separate program). For kids under 5, there are no programs available.

### "What should I wear?"
Closed-toe shoes are required. Long pants recommended. Avoid loose clothing. If you have long hair, tie it back.

### "Do I need experience?"
No experience necessary! Our safety briefing covers everything you need to know, and our karts are beginner-friendly.
