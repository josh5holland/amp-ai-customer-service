# Concession Karting Session Types

## Public Sessions ("Fastest Lap")

### Overview
- Drop-in karting sessions open to anyone meeting requirements
- No booking required (walk-up OK) but online booking recommended
- Compete against yourself and track "Fastest of the Week" records

### Booking
- **URL:** bookings.clubspeed.com/amp

### Pricing (Always Recommend the 3-Pack!)

| Package | Price | Track Time | Per Session | Savings |
|---------|-------|------------|-------------|---------|
| 1 Session | $35.99 | 8 minutes | $35.99 | - |
| **3 Sessions (MOST POPULAR)** | $87.99 | 24 minutes | $29.33 | Save ~$20 |
| 5 Sessions | $129.99 | 40 minutes | $26.00 | Save ~$50 |

**Sales tip:** Always mention the 3-pack as "most popular" - it's better value and gets them more track time to actually improve.

### Best For
- Individuals
- Small groups (2-6 people)
- Families
- Spontaneous visits
- First-time karters

---

## League Racing

### Overview
- Multi-week competitive series
- Points accumulated over the season
- Regular schedule of races
- Standings and championship tracking

### Booking
- **URL:** motorsportreg.com/orgs/atlanta-motorsports-park/events
- Check for current league schedules

### Best For
- Regular visitors who want structured competition
- People who like ongoing commitment
- Those wanting to improve through competition
- Building karting community

---

## Team Events / Enduros

### Overview
- Extended races lasting 1-3 hours
- Teams of 2-4 drivers
- Driver changes during the race (pit stops)
- Team strategy element

### Booking
- **URL:** motorsportreg.com/orgs/atlanta-motorsports-park/events
- Check for upcoming enduro events

### Best For
- Friend groups
- Corporate teams
- Birthday parties wanting competitive element
- People who want longer track time

---

## Corporate / Group Events / Birthday Parties

### Overview
- Grand Prix Package: practice, qualifying, final race, podium celebration
- Can include private room rental, catering, and award ceremony
- **AMP is closed Mondays and Tuesdays**

### How to Handle
Use the **template approach** - see `knowledge/group-event-template.md`

1. Collect: day of week + number of guests
2. Send the full template response with calculated pricing
3. Route follow-up questions to jessica@atlantamotorsportspark.com

### Best For
- Birthday parties
- Corporate team building
- Client entertainment
- Celebrations and milestones

## Junior Discovery Experience

### Overview
- Kids' karting academy for ages 5-11
- 2.5-hour structured program
- Beginner cone course (not full track)
- Everything provided

### Details
- **Ages:** 5-11 years old
- **Duration:** 2.5 hours
- **Cost:** $249
- **Includes:** Kart, helmet, racing suit, snacks, water
- **What they learn:** Karting safety, kart controls, beginner cone course

### Booking
- **URL:** motorsportreg.com
- **Phone:** (678) 381-8526
- **More info:** https://www.atlantamotorsportspark.com/junior-discovery-experience/

### Best For
- Kids too young for rental karts (under 12)
- Families wanting to introduce kids to karting
- No-commitment first experience
- Kids interested in racing but parents unsure

## Session Selection Guide

| Situation | Recommended Session |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Just want to try karting | Public Session |
| Group of friends (< 10) | Public Session, same time |
| Want structured competition | League Racing |
| Corporate team building | Group Event / Private |
| Kids 5-11 | Junior Discovery Experience |
| Looking for longer experience | Team Enduro |
| Regular visitor | League Racing |
