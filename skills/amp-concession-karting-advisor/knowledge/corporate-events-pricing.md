# Corporate Karting Events - Complete Pricing Guide

## Overview

AMP offers structured corporate karting experiences with the **Grand Prix Package** being the most popular choice. Events can include private room rental, catering, and award ceremonies.

**Important:** AMP is closed Mondays and Tuesdays for corporate events.

---

## Grand Prix Package (Most Popular)

### What's Included
- Two 8-minute practice sessions
- One 8-minute qualifying session
- 10-lap final race
- Podium celebration for top 3 drivers

### Pricing

| Day | Per Person | Minimum Spend | Min Guests |
|-----|------------|---------------|------------|
| Wednesday/Thursday | $179 | $1,790 | 10 |
| Friday/Saturday/Sunday | $209 | $4,200 | 20 |

## Add-On Services

### Room Rental
- **Kart Track Private Room:** $450
- Ideal for gathering before/after racing
- Required for buffet service

### Award Ceremony
- **Cost:** $75
- Podium celebration with trophies/recognition

### Catering Options

#### For Groups Under 20: Unlimited Snacks & Beverages
- **Cost:** $18 per person
- Light refreshments and drinks

#### For Groups 20+: Unlimited Coca-Cola & Beverages
- **Cost:** $10 per person
- Soft drinks and beverages

#### For Groups 20+: Buffet Service
- **Cost:** ~$28 per person (varies by menu selection)
- Full meal service
- Multiple menu options available
- Requires private room rental

## Booking Process

1. Customer contacts AMP with group size and preferred date
2. AMP provides custom quote based on requirements
3. Quote valid for specific date/time
4. Deposit required to confirm booking

---

## Key Points for Quoting

- **Tax Rate:** 8%
- **Minimums:** Always communicate day-specific minimums
- **Closed Days:** Mondays and Tuesdays
- **Buffet:** Price varies by menu selection (~$28/person is estimate)
- **Room Rental:** Recommended for all catered events
- **Award Ceremony:** Optional but popular add-on
