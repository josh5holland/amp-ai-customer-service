# Handoff Response Templates

## Safety Concern Handoff

```
Thank you for bringing this to our attention - safety is our top priority and we take this very seriously.

I'm immediately escalating this to our management team. Here's what happens next:

1. A member of our team will contact you within 24 hours
2. They'll want to hear the full details
3. We'll investigate and take appropriate action

Can you confirm the best email and phone number to reach you?

In the meantime, you can reach out directly to shawn@atlantamotorsportspark.com.

Thank you for caring about our community's safety.
```

---

## Angry Customer Handoff

```
I hear your frustration, and I'm sorry you're having this experience. You deserve better.

I'm connecting you with our team immediately:

**Contact:** shawn@atlantamotorsportspark.com
**Subject:** Urgent - Member Concern

I'm noting your message so they have context before reaching out. Is there anything specific you'd like me to highlight for them?

We want to make this right.
```

---

## Refund Request Handoff

```
I'm sorry your experience didn't meet expectations - that's not the standard we aim for.

Refund requests are handled by our team directly:

**Email:** info@atlantamotorsportspark.com
**Subject:** Refund Request - [Date of Visit]

Please include:
- Your name and contact info
- Date and time of your visit
- Booking confirmation if available
- Description of what happened

They'll review your case and respond within 2-3 business days.

Is there anything else I can help you document?
```

## Corporate Event Handoff

```
That sounds like a great event! For [custom needs], you'll want to work directly with our events team.

Please reach out to:
**Email:** shawn@atlantamotorsportspark.com
**Subject:** Corporate Event Inquiry - [# of people]

Include:
- Preferred date(s)
- Activities you're interested in
- Any catering or meeting space needs
- Special requests

They can create a custom package for you.
```

## Previous Issue Follow-Up Handoff

```
I see you've been in contact about this before. Let me make sure we get this resolved properly.

I'm connecting you with our team:
**Email:** shawn@atlantamotorsportspark.com
**Subject:** Follow-up on Previous Issue

I'm including your conversation history so you don't have to repeat everything. They should reach out within [timeframe].

I apologize this hasn't been resolved yet.
```

---

## Template Variables

When using templates, replace:
- `[Date of Visit]` - Customer's visit date
- `[# of people]` - Group size
- `[custom needs]` - Specific requirements
- `[topic]` - Subject of question
- `[appropriate email]` - Based on routing table
- `[timeframe]` - Based on urgency
- `[Publication/Outlet Name]` - Media source
