# Road Course Membership Overview

## Quick Summary

- **What:** 2-mile, 16-turn road circuit for cars and motorcycles
- **Membership:** Required ($25,000 - $100,000 initiation)
- **Focus:** Community, hobby driving, not primarily competition
- **For:** Automotive enthusiasts wanting a home track

---

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | Initiation | Monthly | Weekend Days/Year |
|------|------------|---------|-------------------|
| Platinum Individual | $25,000 | $299 | 24 (~2/month) |
| Diamond Individual | $50,000 | $299 | 48 (~4/month) |
| Diamond + Family | $60,000 | $399 | 48 (includes family) |
| Corporate Diamond | $100,000 | $899 | 48 (up to 4 drivers) |

---

## What's Included

- Member track days (Thursday, Friday, alternating weekends)
- Monthly classroom instruction
- 5+ Track Day Socials per year
- Holiday party and member events
- Guest privileges

---

## Competition (Optional)

- Autocross (cone course, street-legal cars)
- Time Attack (timed laps)
- Club Racing (wheel-to-wheel, caged race cars)
- 2026 Championship: $1,000 prize per class winner

**Note:** Competition is optional. Most members drive for fun.

---

## Non-Member Options

The road course is membership-based, but non-members can:
- Attend public events/driving schools
- Come as a member's guest
- Check: https://www.atlantamotorsportspark.com/events/
