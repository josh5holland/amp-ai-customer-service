# AMP Membership Comparison

## Quick Reference Table

| Feature | Road Course | Race Karting | Concession Karting |
|---------|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Vehicle** | Cars/Motorcycles | Personal race karts | Rental Sodi karts |
| **Track** | 2-mile road circuit | Kart track | Kart track |

| **Entry cost** | $25,000+ | $3,500+ | Per session |

## Road Course Membership

### What It Is
- Access to AMP's 2-mile, 16-turn road circuit
- For cars and motorcycles
- Membership-based (not pay-per-session)

### Cost
| Tier | Initiation | Monthly |
|------|------------|---------|
| Platinum Individual | $25,000 | $299 |
| Diamond Individual | $50,000 | $299 |
| Diamond + Family | $60,000 | $399 |
| Corporate Diamond | $100,000 | $899 |

### Best For
- Automotive enthusiasts
- Track day hobbyists
- Corporate entertainment
- Families who drive together

### Route To
**amp-main-track-advisor** for detailed information

---

## Race Karting Membership

### What It Is
- Access to AMP's kart track with your own race kart
- For competitive karting, ages 5+
- Membership required

### Cost
| Type | Initiation | Monthly | Daily |
|------|------------|---------|-------|
| Standard | $3,500 | $95 | $20 |
| Standard Add-on | $2,000 | $55 | $20 |
| Lifetime | $10,000 | None | None |
| Lifetime Add-on | $5,500 | None | None |

**Plus equipment:** ~$5,500 for beginner setup

### Best For
- Competitive racers
- Parents with racing kids (ages 5+)
- Motorsports enthusiasts
- Long-term commitment

### Route To
**amp-karting-advisor** for detailed information

---

## Concession Karting (No Membership)

### What It Is
- Rent AMP's Sodi karts for a session
- No membership required
- Walk-up or book online

### Cost
- Approximately $35/session
- Packages available for multiple sessions

### Requirements
- Age 12+
- Height 60+ inches
- Closed-toe shoes

### Best For
- Casual fun
- One-time visits
- Groups and parties
- Entertainment

### Route To
**amp-concession-karting-advisor** for booking help

---

## For Kids Under 12

### Junior Discovery Experience
- Ages 5-11
- $249 for 2.5-hour program
- Everything provided
- No membership needed
- Great first introduction

### Race Karting Path
- Ages 5+
- Own equipment required
- Membership required
- Competitive focus

## Decision Guide

### "I want to drive my car/motorcycle on track"
→ Road Course Membership ($25,000+)

### "I want to own a race kart and compete"
→ Race Karting Membership ($3,500+)

### "I want to rent a kart for fun"
→ Concession Karting (no membership)

### "My young child wants to try karting"
→ Junior Discovery Experience ($249) or Race Karting Membership (age 5+)

---

## Common Misconceptions

| What They Say | What They Probably Mean |
|---------------|-------------------------|
| "Karting membership to drive my car" | Road Course Membership |
| "Membership to rent karts" | No membership needed (concession) |
| "How much is membership?" | Depends - ask what they want to do |
| "Kids membership" | Race Karting or Junior Discovery |
