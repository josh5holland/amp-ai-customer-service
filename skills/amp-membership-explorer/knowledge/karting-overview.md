# Karting Programs Overview

## Two Different Karting Options

AMP has TWO completely separate karting programs. This confuses many people.

---

## Race Karting Membership

### What It Is
- For people who OWN their own race kart
- Membership REQUIRED
- Competitive racing focus
- Ages 5+ (no maximum)

### Cost
- Standard: $3,500 initiation + $95/month + $20/day
- Lifetime: $10,000 one-time (no monthly/daily fees)
- Plus equipment: ~$5,500 minimum

### Best For
- Serious competitors
- Kids interested in racing (ages 5+)
- Long-term motorsports involvement

### Route To
**amp-karting-advisor** for detailed questions

---

## Concession Karting (No Membership)

### What It Is
- Rent AMP's Sodi karts for a session
- NO membership required
- Entertainment/fun focus
- Ages 12+, 60 inches tall

### Cost
- ~$35 per session
- Everything provided (kart, helmet)

### Best For
- Casual fun
- One-time visits
- Groups and parties
- No commitment

### Route To
**amp-concession-karting-advisor** for booking help

---

## For Young Kids (Ages 5-11)

### Junior Discovery Experience
- $249 for 2.5-hour program
- Everything provided
- Great introduction
- No commitment

### Race Karting Path
- Starts at age 5
- Requires membership + equipment
- Competitive focus

---

## Quick Decision

| If they want to... | Direct to... |
|-------------------|--------------|
| Rent karts casually | Concession (no membership) |
| Own a kart and compete | Race Karting Membership |
| Get serious about racing | Race Karting Membership |
| Just try karting once | Concession or Junior Discovery |
