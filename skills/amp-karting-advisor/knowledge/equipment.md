# Karting Equipment Guide

## Beginner Equipment Costs (LO206 Setup)

| Item | Approximate Cost | Notes |
|------|------------------|-------|
| New chassis | ~$3,000 | Can find used for less |
| Engine (LO206) | ~$850 | Briggs & Stratton 4-stroke |
| Safety gear | ~$1,200 | Helmet, suit, gloves, rib protector |
| Wheels/tires | ~$500 | Consumable - ongoing cost |
| **Total Setup** | **~$5,550** | Minimum to get started |

---

## Detailed Equipment Breakdown

### Kart Chassis
- **New:** $3,000-$5,000+
- **Used:** $1,500-$3,000
- **Top brands:** OTK (Tony Kart, FA Kart), Birel ART, CRG, CompKart

**Recommendation:** Work with AMP Kartwerks (on-site pro shop) to find the right chassis for your class and budget

### Engines

| Type | Cost (New) | Maintenance | Best For |
|------|------------|-------------|----------|
| LO206 | ~$850 | Low | Beginners |
| IAME X30 | ~$3,500 | Medium | Intermediate |
| Rotax | ~$4,000+ | Medium | Intermediate |
| Shifter (KZ) | ~$6,000+ | High | Advanced |

### Safety Gear Requirements

| Item | Cost Range | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| Helmet | $300-$700 | Snell SA rated required |
| Racing suit | $200-$500 | SFI or CIK rated |
| Gloves | $50-$150 | Karting-specific recommended |
| Rib protector | $150-$300 | Essential for all ages |
| Neck brace | $50-$150 | Required for minors |
| Racing shoes | $100-$200 | Optional but recommended |

---

## Ongoing Costs

### Per-Session Consumables
- Tires (when worn): $150-$300/set
- Fuel (LO206): ~$4/gallon pump gas
- Fuel (two-stroke): ~$15-25/gallon race fuel

### Maintenance Schedule
- **LO206:** Engine rebuild every 2-3 seasons with regular use
- **Two-stroke:** More frequent maintenance, depends on hours

## Budget Tiers

### Minimum Budget (~$9,000 first year)
- Used chassis: ~$2,000
- LO206 engine: ~$850
- Basic safety gear: ~$700
- Standard membership: ~$3,500 + dues
- Consumables: ~$500

### Comfortable Budget (~$15,000 first year)
- New chassis: ~$4,000
- LO206 engine: ~$850
- Quality safety gear: ~$1,200
- Standard membership: ~$3,500 + dues
- Spare parts/consumables: ~$1,000

### Premium Budget (~$25,000+ first year)
- Premium chassis: ~$5,000
- TaG engine: ~$3,500
- Premium safety gear: ~$1,500
- Lifetime membership: ~$10,000
- Full spare parts kit: ~$2,000

## Try Before You Buy

**Before investing in equipment, consider:**

1. **Junior Discovery Experience** ($249) - Ages 5-11, no commitment
2. **Karting School** ($549) - Full day, all equipment provided
3. **Private Lessons** (~$275/hour) - Personalized instruction

This lets you experience karting for under $600 before making the $5,000+ equipment investment.
