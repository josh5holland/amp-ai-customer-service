# Race Karting Membership Pricing

## Standard Karting Membership

### First Member
- **Initiation:** $3,500
- **Monthly dues:** $95/month
- **Daily track fee:** $20/day

### Family Add-On (2nd family member)
- **Initiation:** $2,000
- **Monthly dues:** $55/month
- **Daily track fee:** $20/day

**Best for:** Testing the waters, uncertain long-term commitment, first-time motorsports families

---

## Lifetime Karting Membership

### First Member
- **Initiation:** $10,000
- **Monthly dues:** None
- **Daily track fee:** None

### Family Add-On (2nd family member)
- **Initiation:** $5,500
- **Monthly dues:** None
- **Daily track fee:** None

**Best for:** Long-term commitment, frequent usage (5+ years), multiple family members
**Break-even:** Approximately 4-5 years of regular use

---

## Upgrade Path

- Standard members can upgrade to Lifetime within their first year
- Original initiation fee ($3,500) credited toward Lifetime ($10,000)
- Net upgrade cost: $6,500

---

## Decision Framework

| If they... | Recommend... | Why |
|------------|--------------|-----|
| Are new to motorsports | Standard | Test commitment first |
| Plan 5+ years of karting | Lifetime | Better value long-term |
| Drive 2+ times/month | Lifetime | Daily fees add up quickly |
| Have multiple family members | Lifetime + Add-on | Significant savings |
| Are uncertain about frequency | Standard | Flexibility to walk away |
