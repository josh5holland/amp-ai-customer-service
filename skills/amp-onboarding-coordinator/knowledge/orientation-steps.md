# New Member Orientation Steps

## Road Course Members

### Step 1: Membership Confirmation
- Membership paperwork completed
- Payment processed
- Welcome email received
- Member credentials issued

### Step 2: Portal Access
- Log into theclub.atlantamotorsportspark.com
- Set up your profile
- Explore the member area
- Find the calendar

### Step 3: Review Resources
- Read member handbook/rules
- Understand flag signals
- Learn track layout (if available)
- Note important contacts

### Step 4: Plan First Visit
- Check member calendar
- Pick a track day
- Register if required
- Note what to bring

### Step 5: First Track Day
- Arrive early
- Complete tech inspection
- Attend drivers meeting
- Start slow, build up

### Step 6: Ongoing Development
- Attend monthly classroom instruction
- Join Track Day Socials
- Consider competition when ready
- Engage with the community

---

## Race Karting Members

### Step 1: Membership Confirmation
- Membership paperwork completed
- Payment processed
- Welcome email received
- Member credentials issued

### Step 2: Training (For New Karters)

**Option A: Already Own Kart**
- Karting School recommended ($549)
- Private lesson recommended (~$275/hour)

**Option B: Need to Buy Kart**
- Karting School first (use their equipment)
- Work with AMP Kartwerks after
- Private lesson with your new kart

### Step 3: Equipment Setup
- Acquire kart and safety gear
- Arrange storage (if needed)
- Get fitted properly for rib protector

### Step 4: Portal Access
- Log into member portal
- Find karting calendar
- Identify practice days

### Step 5: Practice Period
- Attend practice days
- Focus on consistency
- Get comfortable with kart and track
- Recommended: 5-10 practice days before racing

### Step 6: First Race
- Enter a club racing event
- Focus on finishing, not winning
- Learn race craft (starts, passing)
- Debrief and improve

## Success Metrics

### You're On Track When:
- You know where to find the calendar
- You've completed your first track day
- You know 2-3 other members by name
- You're planning your next visit
- You're enjoying the experience!

### Signs You Need Help:
- Confused about next steps
- Portal access issues
- Equipment questions
- Feeling overwhelmed
- Any concerns or frustrations

**Don't hesitate to reach out** - that's what the community is for!
