# First Visit Guide

## What to Expect

Your first track day at AMP is exciting! Here's what a typical day looks like.

---

## Before You Arrive

### Road Course
- [ ] Confirm your track day registration (if required)
- [ ] Check vehicle: brakes, fluids, tire condition, battery secure
- [ ] Remove loose items from car interior
- [ ] Pack: helmet, closed-toe shoes, sunscreen, water
- [ ] Review track map if available
- [ ] Get good rest the night before

### Race Karting
- [ ] Confirm your practice session
- [ ] If bringing kart: check condition, have tools
- [ ] If using school kart: confirm arrangement
- [ ] Pack: helmet, rib protector, gloves, water
- [ ] Wear: closed-toe shoes, long pants recommended

---

## When You Arrive

### Road Course Timeline
1. **Arrive early** (30+ minutes before session)
2. **Find registration/check-in**
3. **Sign waiver** (if not done online)
4. **Unload and prep vehicle** in paddock
5. **Go through tech inspection**
6. **Attend drivers meeting** (mandatory)
7. **Wait for your run group assignment**

### Race Karting Timeline
1. **Arrive early** (30+ minutes)
2. **Check in** and sign waiver
3. **Unload kart** (or get assigned equipment)
4. **Prep kart** in pit area
5. **Attend safety briefing**
6. **Wait for track to open**

---

## During Your Session

### Road Course
- **First laps:** Go slow! Learn the track layout
- **Point-by passing:** Raise hand to let faster cars pass
- **Obey flags:** Yellow = caution, Black = come in
- **Use mirrors:** Be aware of traffic around you
- **Take breaks:** Don't exhaust yourself

### Race Karting
- **Start conservative:** Learn the line before pushing
- **Watch for flags:** Safety first
- **Respect others:** Clean driving only
- **Take breaks:** Fatigue affects judgment

---

## After Your Session

### Road Course
- Cool down your car (don't immediately turn off)
- Check for any issues (leaks, damage, loose items)
- Talk to other members - this is a community!
- Plan your next visit

### Race Karting
- Cool down
- Check kart condition
- Load up equipment
- Debrief - what went well? What to improve?

---

## Tips for First-Timers

### Mindset
- **Goal is learning, not lap times** - Focus on consistency
- **Everyone was a beginner** - Don't be embarrassed to be new
- **Ask questions** - Members and staff are friendly
- **Start slow, build up** - Speed comes with experience

### Social
- **Introduce yourself** - "Hi, I'm new here"
- **Watch experienced drivers** - You can learn a lot
- **Stay for socials** - Community is big at AMP
- **Exchange contact info** - Build your track day network

### Safety
- **When in doubt, slow down**
- **If something feels wrong, come in**
- **Follow staff instructions immediately**
- **No heroics on day one**

---

## Common First-Day Mistakes

| Mistake | Better Approach |
|---------|-----------------|
| Arriving late | Arrive 30+ min early |
| Pushing too hard early | Start at 50-60%, build up |
| Skipping drivers meeting | Mandatory - always attend |
| Ignoring flags | Learn flags before you go |
| Not drinking water | Stay hydrated! |
| Driving when exhausted | Take breaks as needed |

---

## Key Contacts On Site

- **Registration desk** - Check-in, waivers, questions
- **Tech inspection** - Vehicle/kart safety check
- **Corner workers** - Flag signals, track safety
- **Staff members** - General help and questions

If lost or confused, just ask! Everyone remembers being new.
