# Event Types at AMP

## Road Course Events

### Member Track Days
- **What:** Regular practice days for members
- **Schedule:** Thursday, Friday, alternating weekends
- **Requirements:** Road course membership
- **Format:** Open track, point-by passing

### Track Day Socials
- **What:** Casual driving combined with social gathering
- **Schedule:** 5+ events per year
- **Requirements:** Road course membership
- **Format:** Relaxed pace, community focused

### Club Racing
- **What:** Wheel-to-wheel competition
- **Requirements:** Membership + race-prepared car + safety gear
- **Format:** Qualifying, races, points series
- **Note:** Requires caged race car and full safety equipment

### Time Attack
- **What:** Timed lap competition against the clock
- **Requirements:** Membership + approved vehicle
- **Format:** Hot laps, best time wins class
- **Note:** Less safety requirements than club racing

### Autocross
- **What:** Cone course competition
- **Requirements:** Membership + street-legal car
- **Format:** One car at a time, cone penalties
- **Note:** Great entry point for competition

### Public Events
- **What:** Driving schools, special experiences
- **Requirements:** None (open to public)
- **Booking:** https://www.atlantamotorsportspark.com/events/
- **Note:** Great way to try before membership

## Karting Events

### Member Practice Days
- **What:** Open track for race kart owners
- **Schedule:** Regular member days
- **Requirements:** Race karting membership + own kart
- **Format:** Practice, testing, development

### Karting Club Racing
- **What:** Competitive kart racing
- **Requirements:** Race karting membership
- **Format:** Points series, multiple classes
- **Note:** 2026 Championship - 5 rounds throughout the year

### Public Karting Sessions
- **What:** Rental kart sessions
- **Requirements:** Age 12+, 60" tall
- **Booking:** bookings.clubspeed.com/amp
- **Format:** Timed sessions, "Fastest of the Week"

### League Racing
- **What:** Multi-week rental kart series
- **Requirements:** Age 12+, 60" tall
- **Booking:** motorsportreg.com/orgs/atlanta-motorsports-park/events
- **Format:** Points accumulated over season

### Team Enduros
- **What:** Extended team races (1-3 hours)
- **Requirements:** Age 12+, 60" tall
- **Booking:** motorsportreg.com/orgs/atlanta-motorsports-park/events
- **Format:** Teams of 2-4, driver changes

### Junior Discovery Experience
- **What:** Kids' karting academy
- **Requirements:** Ages 5-11
- **Cost:** $249 for 2.5 hours
- **Booking:** motorsportreg.com or (678) 381-8526
- **Format:** Learning, cone course, everything provided

## Special Events

### Monthly Classroom Instruction
- **What:** Educational sessions for members
- **Cost:** Included with membership
- **Topics:** Driving technique, car setup, safety

### Holiday Party
- **What:** Annual member celebration
- **Requirements:** Membership
- **Format:** End-of-year community event

### Corporate Events
- **What:** Private events for businesses
- **Contact:** AMP directly for custom arrangements
- **Options:** Track time, karting, catering
