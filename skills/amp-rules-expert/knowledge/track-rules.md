# Track Rules

## On-Track Behavior

### Flag Compliance
All drivers MUST obey flag signals immediately:
- **Green:** Track is clear, continue racing
- **Yellow:** Caution - no passing, reduce speed significantly
- **Red:** Stop safely as quickly as possible, session ended
- **Black:** Report to pit immediately - there's an issue
- **Blue:** Faster car behind you - facilitate a safe pass
- **White:** Slow vehicle on track ahead
- **Checkered:** Session complete, cool-down lap

### Passing Rules
Rules vary by event type:
- **Track days:** Point-by passing (faster car can only pass when slower car points them by)
- **Competition:** May allow passing without point-by in designated zones
- Always check event-specific passing rules

### Communication
- Follow corner worker instructions without question
- Radio communication rules vary by event
- No cell phone use while on track

### Incident Response
- If you go off track, rejoin safely when clear
- If you have a mechanical issue, get off racing line immediately
- If involved in incident, follow staff instructions

---

## Paddock / Pit Rules

### Speed Limits
- Strictly enforced speed limits in pit lane and paddock
- Walking speed in congested areas
- No racing in the paddock - ever

### General Conduct
- No reckless driving
- Yield to pedestrians
- Follow directional signs

---

## Prohibited Actions

- Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Aggressive driving toward other vehicles
- Ignoring flag signals
- Blocking faster traffic intentionally
- Entering track without proper credentials/registration
- Unapproved vehicle modifications

---

## Noise Compliance

- AMP has noise restrictions that vary by event
- Some track days have stricter limits
- Vehicles may be tested for compliance
- Excessively loud vehicles may be excluded

---

## Consequences

Rule violations may result in:
- Warning
- Session exclusion
- Event exclusion
- Membership suspension
- Permanent ban (severe violations)

**Safety rules are not negotiable.** The goal is for everyone to go home safely.
