# Safety Gear Requirements

## Helmet Requirements

### Road Course
| Event Type | Minimum Rating | Recommended |
|------------|----------------|-------------|
| Track days / HPDE | Snell SA2015 | Snell SA2020 |
| Time Attack | Snell SA2015 | Snell SA2020 |
| Club Racing | Snell SA2015 | Snell SA2020 |

**Note:** SA = Special Application (motorsports rated, fire-resistant lining)

### Karting
| Activity | Acceptable Ratings |
|----------|-------------------|
| Race karting | Snell SA, M, or K |
| Concession karting | Provided by AMP |

### Helmet Condition
- No cracks, dents, or visible damage
- Within manufacturer's service life (typically 5-10 years)
- Properly fitting (snug but comfortable)

---

## Racing Suit Requirements

### When Required
- Club racing (wheel-to-wheel) - Required
- Most competition events - Check specific rules
- Track days / HPDE - Recommended, not required
- Race karting - Recommended for racing

### Specifications
- SFI or FIA rated
- One-piece or two-piece (check event rules)
- Fire-resistant material
- Good condition (no tears or damage)

---

## Footwear Requirements

### All Activities
- **Closed-toe shoes required** - No exceptions
- Sandals, flip-flops, and open-toe shoes are NOT allowed

### Competition
- Racing shoes/boots recommended
- Must cover ankle for club racing

---

## Additional Safety Gear

### Gloves
- Required for club racing
- Recommended for all activities
- Fire-resistant for competition

### Neck Support (HANS/Neck Restraint)
- Required for club racing
- Recommended for all track driving
- Must be compatible with helmet and harness

### Fire-Resistant Underwear
- Required for club racing
- Highly recommended for competition

---

## Karting-Specific Gear

### Race Karting
| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Helmet | Snell SA, M, or K |
| Rib protector | Required |
| Neck brace | Required for minors |
| Gloves | Recommended |
| Racing suit | Recommended for racing |

### Concession Karting
- Helmet provided by AMP
- Closed-toe shoes required
- Long pants recommended

---

## Where to Get Gear

### AMP Kartwerks (On-Site)
- Helmets
- Racing suits
- Gloves
- Karting-specific gear

### Motorsports Retailers
- National chains (Winding Road Racing, etc.)
- Online retailers
- Make sure ratings meet requirements

## Gear Inspection

- All safety gear may be inspected during tech
- Expired or damaged gear will be rejected
- It's your responsibility to have proper equipment
- When in doubt, upgrade your gear
