# AMP Rules and Regulations (Revised 5/14/2025)

## I. USE OF THE PREMISES

### Risk Acknowledgment
- Motorsports involve considerable risk including injury or death
- Members and guests accept full responsibility for damage, injury, or fatality
- Members must disclose all risks to their guests before entering

### Guest Fees & Limits
- **Main track guest fee:** $275
- **Kart track guest fee:** $100
- **Guest limit:** 2 visits per year (per guest, regardless of host member)
- Passengers are exempt from charges

### Violations
- Driving on non-designated day without registering as guest: Warning → Full fee + 50% admin → Suspension/revocation
- Allowing guest to exceed 2-visit limit: 3-month suspension (first offense) → Up to year/revocation

### Member Responsibilities
- Accompany guests who are less than intermediate/advanced drivers
- Share all rules, regulations, and risks with guests
- Assume full responsibility for guest behavior and safety

---

## II. OPERATING SCHEDULE

### Track Hours
- **Operating hours:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Lunch break (cold track):** 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM (unless altered by private rental)
- **Engine revving:** NOT permitted before 8:45 AM or after 5:00 PM

### RSVP Requirements
- Members must RSVP by 5 PM the evening before
- If arriving after 12 PM, specify in RSVP form
- No RSVPs by 6 PM = track closed next day
- Motorcycles must RSVP before 5 PM to get a run group

### Before/After Hours Track Access
- Walking, running, pedal biking permitted on designated member days
- Get written consent from Race Control or front office
- Be aware of maintenance activities
- Wildlife present - maintain safe distance
- NO motorized vehicles during these times

---

## III. WAIVER REQUIREMENTS

- **Everyone** must sign waiver at the front gate when entering AMP property
- Wristband must be worn at all times
- Sharing wristband = 90-day suspension with dues accruing

### Adults (18+)
- Sign waiver at the gate upon arrival

### Minors (Under 18)

**With Parent/Legal Guardian Present:**
- Parent/guardian signs waiver for minor at the gate
- Must be supervised by adult at all times

**Without Parent/Legal Guardian:**
- Must print and complete the Minor Waiver form before arriving
- Must get waiver notarized
- Must be accompanied by an adult (any person 18+)
- Present notarized waiver to security gate staff

**Minor Waiver Form:** https://www.atlantamotorsportspark.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/Minor-Waiver-Notary.pdf

**Online notarization:** Available at www.notarize.com

---

## IV. DRIVER/RIDER CRITERIA

### Licensing Requirements
- Government/State issued Driver's License or International Driving Permit required
- Keep medical profile on file (blood type, conditions, emergency contacts)

### New Member Requirements
- Must complete AMP orientation and instruction class before first track use

### Road Course - Adults (18+)
Must be signed off for solo driving via one of:
- Racing license from: Primal Racing School, SCCA, NASA, Skip Barber, Porsche Track Experience, etc.
- Sign-off from certified instructor (SCCA, NASA, PCA, BMW CCA, Chin, Legends, etc.)

### Road Course - Minors (13-17)
Same requirements as adults, plus:
- Fully executed Minor Release and Waiver
- May use kart racing license + car instructor sign-off

### Karting Course
- Members under 18 must be accompanied by parent/guardian
- Parent responsible for supervision entering/exiting track and paddock
- Parent should quiz minor on flags, safety, etiquette each day

---

## V. VEHICLE INSPECTION

- Members responsible for vehicle upkeep and safety
- Current Tech Inspection Sticker required (front windshield area)
- After incident (off-track, spin, collision): return for re-inspection
- Karts: maintain per World Karting Association (WKA) rules

### Refueling
- Vehicle must be OFF
- No smoking
- Report oil/fluid spills immediately
- Cleanup fee if not notified promptly

---

## VI. SAFETY GEAR

### All Cars
- Helmets: Snell SA2015 or SA2020
- Must have current AMP "helmet approved sticker"
- Fire extinguisher: highly recommended
- Head/neck restraints: strongly advised
- Closed-toe shoes required

### Production Cars
- 5 or 6-point harnesses and rollover protection: strongly recommended
- SFI-rated racing suits: highly recommended

### Race Cars
- 5 or 6-point harnesses and rollover protection: strongly advised
- SFI-rated suits, gloves, racing shoes: highly recommended

### Karts
- Helmets: SA2010 or later required
- **Racing conditions required:** CIK/FIA rated suits or jeans with jacket, shoes, gloves, neck brace, rib protection
- **Non-racing recommended:** Long sleeves, long pants, helmet, neck brace
- Helmets required when moving through paddock

### Motorcycles
- Helmets: M90 or later required
- FIM-rated suits, race boots, leather jacket, gloves, back protection required

### Open Cockpit / Convertibles
- Roll bar or factory roll-over protection mandatory
- "Broomstick" test recommended

### Cameras
- NO helmet-mounted GoPros
- Visor-mounted cameras OK
- Vehicle/kart mounted OK (owner assumes liability if detaches)

## VII. FLAGS AND SIGNALS

| Flag | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Green** | Track clear, proceed with caution |
| **Steady Yellow** | Caution, no passing until next corner station |
| **Waving Yellow** | Increased caution, be prepared to stop, no passing |
| **Double Yellow** | Full-course yellow, no passing, max 50 mph |
| **White w/ Red Cross** | Medical vehicle on track - CAUTION |
| **Red** | SESSION HALTED - proceed slowly to next corner, stop, wait |
| **Black (rolled, pointed at you)** | You are warned for violating regulations |
| **Black (fully flown)** | Proceed to pit road, consult Pit-Out Marshal |
| **Black Flag ALL** | Everyone return to pits slowly, await instructions |
| **White** | Slow-moving vehicle ahead |
| **Blue w/ Yellow Band** | Faster vehicle approaching - maintain line, prepare to point by |
| **Yellow w/ Red Stripes** | Slippery conditions or debris (always stationary) |
| **Black w/ Orange Ball (Meatball)** | Mechanical issue - go directly to pits, NO more laps |
| **Checkered** | Session ended, exit next lap |

---

## VIII. PASSING RULES

- Overtaking only in designated zones with point-by or blinkers
- Watch mirrors - if faster car behind for multiple turns, facilitate pass
- **Novice/Intermediate:** No corner passing, only after point-by
- Overtaking driver responsible for safe, clean pass
- Complete pass before corner entrance

---

## IX. INCIDENTS & STRIKES

### Off-Track / Spin Procedures
- **All wheels off:** Slow down, rejoin gently, go to pits for inspection
- **Spin on track:** Regain control, proceed to pits, explain to Marshal
- **Disabled vehicle:** Steer off track opposite racing line, STAY IN VEHICLE (exit only for fire)

### Strike System
| Incident | Strikes |
|----------|---------|
| 2-Wheels Off or Spin | 1 strike |
| 4-Wheels Off | 2 strikes |
| Missed Flag | 1 strike per station |
| Drifting (2+ connected corners) | 1 strike |
| Motorcycle highside/lowside/collision | 3 strikes |

### Penalties
- **1st Strike:** Report to stewards, continue
- **2nd Strike:** 30-minute cooldown
- **3rd Strike:** Done for the day

## X. NOISE LIMITS (Strictly Enforced)

| Track | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Main Road Course | 98 dB (A-weighted, 50ft from edge) |
| Kart Track / Autocross | 92 dB (A-weighted, 50ft from edge) |

### Kart Requirements
- Air box required
- Fully functional muffler/silencer required

### Violations
- 1st: Black flag until exhaust modified
- 2nd (same day): Black flag, done for day
- 3rd (within 30 days): 60-day suspension, dues continue
- 4th (within 60 days): 120-day suspension, dues continue
- Intentional deception (2x): Revocation + potential $1000 fine

## XI. PADDOCK MOBILITY

- Allowed: Scooters, bikes, side-by-sides, golf carts
- Operators must be 16+ years old
- Speed limit: 15 mph
- Stay on roadways and paddock areas
- Helmets required for scooters and bikes
- NO track access (track is for walking/fitness only)

---

## XII. PARKING LOT RULES

- All stands (motorcycles, motorhomes) need blocks under them
- **No staking on asphalt** - $350 fine per hole
- Canopies/tents: weight-based securing only

---

## XIII. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Firearms/weapons:** Prohibited
- **Fireworks:** Not allowed
- **Campfires:** Not permitted
- **Glass containers:** Prohibited
- **Tires/fluids:** Must be removed from premises ($20/tire fine, cleanup fee for fluids)
- **Fuel storage in garages:** Only in certified fuel cabinet
- **Leave quietly:** Drive through Dawsonville courteously, within speed limits

## XIV. SKID PAD / DDC

- Not included in membership
