# Road Course Schedule & Events

## Regular Member Track Days

### Weekday Access (All Tiers)
- **Thursday:** Member track day
- **Friday:** Member track day

### Weekend Access (Varies by Tier)
- Alternating weekends throughout the year
- **Platinum:** 24 weekend days/year (~2/month)
- **Diamond:** 48 weekend days/year (~4/month)

---

## Included Member Events

### Monthly Classroom Instruction
- Included at no extra cost with all memberships
- Topics rotate: driving technique, car setup, safety
- Great for continuous improvement

### Track Day Socials
- 5+ events per year
- Casual driving + social atmosphere
- Food and community gathering

### Holiday Party
- Annual member celebration
- End-of-year recognition
- Community bonding event

---

## Competition Calendar (Optional)

### 2026 Championship
- **Format:** 5 rounds throughout the year
- **Prize:** $1,000 per class winner
- **Eligibility:** Active members

### Event Types Available
| Event | Description | Frequency |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Autocross | Cone course, street-legal cars | Monthly |
| Time Attack | Timed laps, track-prepped cars | Periodic |
| Club Racing | Wheel-to-wheel, caged race cars | Series events |

**Note:** Competition is optional. Many members never compete and just enjoy regular track days.
