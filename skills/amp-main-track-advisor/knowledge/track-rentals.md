# Main Track Rentals

## Overview

AMP's main circuit is available for private rental by organizations, clubs, and businesses. Track rentals are ideal for HPDE events, racing series, corporate driving experiences, film/photo shoots, and manufacturer testing.

**For rental inquiries, contact AMP directly** - pricing varies by season and day of week.

---

## What's Included in a Track Rental

- Full use of the 2-mile, 16-turn road course
- 1 paddock area
- 4 corner marshals
- 1 pit-out marshal
- Race control
- Tow truck
- Staffed fire truck + ambulance
- 1 indoor space
- Event support staff (custodial, front gate, office)

---

## Track Facilities

### Circuits
- 2-mile, 16-turn road course
- Multiple layout configurations available
- Well-maintained racing surface

### Wet Skidpad & Ice Hill
- 7-degree slope on Ice Hill
- Perimeter water jets on 2 circles
- 2 water gates on the Ice Hill
- Great for driver training and car control exercises

### Paddock Area
- South Paddock available for participant staging
- Sample HPDE/race layouts available on request

### Conference Center
- Indoor meeting space
- Catering available
- Virtual walkthrough available on request

---

## Amenities On-Site

- **Country club vibe** - Well-maintained facilities and grounds
- **EV charging** - Level 2 and Level 3 chargers on-site
- **Fuel station** - Self-serve with regular and race fuels
- **Restrooms and showers** - Available for participants
- **Catering** - Menu options available for events

---

## Track Hours & Rules

- **Track hot hours:** Generally 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM with a 1-hour lunch break
- **Sound limit:** 98 dB max measured from track edge (strictly enforced)
- **No engine revving** before 8:45 AM or after 5:00 PM
- Overtime hours available (additional fees apply)

## Booking Requirements

- **Deposit:** 50% due with signed contract (check, wire, or bank transfer)
- **Balance:** Due 7-14 days before event
- **Insurance:** Certificate of Insurance (COI) due 21 days before event
- **TULIP Option:** AMP offers TULIP insurance for those who need it
