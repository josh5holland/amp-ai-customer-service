# Road Course Membership Tiers

## Tier Overview

| Tier | Initiation | Monthly Dues | Weekend Days/Year | Total Days/Year |
|------|------------|--------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Platinum Individual | $25,000 | $299 | 24 (~2/month) | 102 |
| Diamond Individual | $50,000 | $299 | 48 (~4/month) | 153 |
| Diamond + Family | $60,000 | $399 | 48 (~4/month) | 153 |
| Corporate Diamond | $100,000 | $899 | 48 (~4/month) | 153 |

---

## Platinum Individual - $25,000 + $299/month

### Access
- 24 weekend days annually (~2 per month)
- 102 total track days per year
- Weekday access: Thursday, Friday
- Weekend access: Alternating weekends (capped)

### Included Benefits
- Monthly classroom instruction
- 5+ Track Day Socials per year
- Holiday party and member events
- Guest privileges

### Best For
- Regular enthusiasts
- 1-2 weekends per month usage
- First-time track membership
- Testing the waters before higher commitment

---

## Diamond Individual - $50,000 + $299/month

### Access
- 48 weekend days annually (~4 per month)
- 153 total track days per year
- Weekday access: Thursday, Friday
- Weekend access: More weekend availability

### Included Benefits
- All Platinum benefits
- Double the weekend access

### Best For
- Serious enthusiasts
- 3-4+ weekends per month usage
- Maximum flexibility needed
- Regular weekend driver

---

## Diamond + Family - $60,000 + $399/month

### Access
- Same as Diamond Individual
- Coverage extends to family members

### Who's Included
- Spouse
- Children up to 25 years old

### Best For
- Families who drive together
- Multiple drivers in household
- Teaching next generation

---

## Corporate Diamond - $100,000 + $899/month

### Access
- Same as Diamond Individual
- Up to 4 drivers from member company

### Business Benefits
- Client entertainment use
- Team building events
- Multiple executive drivers

### Best For
- Business use
- Client entertainment
- Companies with multiple driving enthusiasts

## Selection Guide

### Choose Platinum if:
- This is your first track membership
- You expect 1-2 weekends per month
- You want to test commitment before upgrading
- Budget is a primary consideration

### Choose Diamond if:
- You want maximum track access
- You expect 3-4+ weekends per month
- Weekend availability is critical
- You're committed long-term

### Choose Diamond + Family if:
- Your spouse also drives
- You have adult children who want track time
- Multiple family members share the hobby

### Choose Corporate if:
- Using track for client entertainment
- Multiple executives need access
- Business expense justification needed
- Team building is a goal
